🍽️ Dining Out Strategies

General Rules

  • ✓ Protein First: Order protein-heavy dishes (grilled chicken, fish, steak, tofu)
  • ✓ Skip the Bread Basket: Save 200-300 calories easily
  • ✓ Sauce on Side: Control hidden calories in dressings/sauces
  • ✓ Vegetable Swap: Replace fries with salad or steamed veggies
  • ✓ Alcohol Strategy: Stick to 1-2 drinks max (wine/spirits over beer)

Safe Bet Meals by Cuisine

Italian 🇮🇹

Grilled fish/chicken, Caprese salad, minestrone soup. Avoid: creamy pastas, pizza.

Asian 🍜

Sashimi, pho (skip noodles), stir-fry with veggies. Avoid: fried rice, tempura.

Mexican 🌮

Fajitas (skip tortillas), ceviche, grilled protein bowls. Avoid: chips, quesadillas.

American 🍔

Bunless burger, grilled chicken salad, steak with veggies. Avoid: fries, milkshakes.

Indian 🍛

Tandoori chicken, dal, veggie curry (less rice). Avoid: naan, samosas, creamy korma.

Office Lunch Scenarios

Unexpected Team Lunch?

  • • Skip breakfast snacks to bank 200-300 calories
  • • Order a "safe bet" from the list above
  • • Eat half portion, save rest for later
  • • Have light dinner (salad + protein shake)
